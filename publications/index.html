Zihao Huang, Yue Wang, Weixing Xin, Xingtong Lin, Huizhen Li, Haowen Chen, Yizhen Lao, Xia Chen
arXiv preprint, 2023

This paper proposes a Siamese neural network integrated with attention mechanisms to tackle few-shot and fine-grained medical image classification. Experimental results on COVID-19 lung samples demonstrate superior robustness and accuracy over standard CNNs under limited data scenarios.
