#1 author 2026 Conference Paper

From 10-K to Industry Multi-Labels: A Bottom-Up LLM-Driven Product Clustering with Pareto Frontier Analysis Abstract This work studies industry multi-labeling from firms' 10-K filings using a bottom-up LLM-driven product clustering pipeline. It frames product-level textual evidence as a way to derive industry labels and uses Pareto frontier analysis to reason about the trade-off between coverage and label quality.

Zihao Huang, Joel Quek, Ke-Wei Huang

#1 author 2025 Conference Paper

Combining LLM-Generated Knowledge Graphs with RAG for Financial Sentiment Extraction Abstract This work integrates LLM-generated knowledge graphs with retrieval-augmented generation for financial sentiment extraction. By transforming financial texts into structured graph representations and retrieving graph-grounded evidence, the framework supports more contextual and evidence-based sentiment assessment.

Zihao Huang, Kelvin Du, Xulang Zhang, Rui Mao, Erik Cambria

#1 author 2023 Preprint

Application of Attention-Based Siamese Composite Neural Network in Medical Image Recognition Abstract This paper proposes an attention-based Siamese composite neural network for few-shot and fine-grained medical image recognition. Experiments on COVID-19 lung samples show that the model is more robust than ordinary neural networks when the number of training images is very small.

Zihao Huang, Yue Wang, Weixing Xin, Xingtong Lin, Huizhen Li, Haowen Chen, Yizhen Lao, Xia Chen
